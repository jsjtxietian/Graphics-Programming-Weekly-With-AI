2019-01-12: [Containerized Shader Compilers](https://www.wihlidal.com/blog/pipeline/2018-12-28-containerized_shader_compilers/)

- supported are: DXC, FXC, GLSLang, AMD Radeon Graphics Analyzer, DXIL signing, SMOL/V and Vulkan SDK
